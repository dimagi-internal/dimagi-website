Acceptable Use Policy

By using Dimagi's Services, you agree not to use the Services in a way that is contrary or inconsistent with the rules and standards published by Dimagi.

Users Shall Not

  • probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of any system or network
  • breach or otherwise circumvent any security or authentication measures
  • access, tamper with, or use non-public areas or parts of the Services
  • interfere with or disrupt any user, host, or network, for example by sending a virus, overloading, flooding, spamming, or mail-bombing
  • access, search, or create accounts for the Services by any means other than our publicly supported interfaces
  • send unsolicited communications, promotions or advertisements, or spam
  • send altered, deceptive or false source-identifying information, including spoofing or phishing
  • promote or advertise products or services other than your own without appropriate authorization
  • abuse referrals or promotions to get more paid features than deserved
  • circumvent the scope of your paid subscription
  • sell the Services unless specifically authorized
  • publish or share materials that are unlawfully pornographic or indecent, or that contain acts of violence
  • use the services to engage in any activity that violates applicable local, national, or international anti-discrimination laws or human rights standards
  • violate the privacy or infringe the rights of others

Enforcement

Any violation of this Policy will result in immediate suspension and/or termination of your access to the Services.
