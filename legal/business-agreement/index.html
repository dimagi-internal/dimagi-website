Introduction

This Dimagi Business Agreement is between Dimagi, Inc. and its affiliate entities (Dimagi Software Innovations Pvt. Ltd., Dimagi South Africa (PTY) LTD) and the business entity or organization agreeing to these terms.

Services

Dimagi will provide the Services as described herein, including appropriate security measures and data processing protections. Dimagi may modify the Services from time to time and will provide reasonable notice of material changes. A software license is granted for use of the Services, and third-party applications available through The Exchange are subject to their own license and end user agreements.

Customer Obligations

The Customer is responsible for administration of the Services and must take reasonable steps to prevent unauthorized use. Customers may not reverse engineer or decompile the Services, and must not violate applicable export control laws. Customers processing protected health information (PHI) must enter into a HIPAA Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with Dimagi. Customers processing EU personal data must enter into a Data Processing Addendum (DPA) as required by GDPR.

Billing and Renewal

Billing is available on monthly or annual plans. Annual subscriptions cancelled within 30 days of billing may be eligible for a refund. Plans auto-renew unless cancelled. Dimagi reserves the right to adjust pricing at any time with reasonable notice. Taxes are the responsibility of the Customer where applicable.

Suspension

Dimagi may suspend the Customer's access for violations of the Acceptable Use Policy or in response to a security emergency. Dimagi will provide reasonable notice of suspension where practicable.

Termination

The Customer may stop using the Services at any time. Upon termination, Customer Data will be available for export for at least two weeks. Data will be deleted within 30 days of a Customer's written deletion request.

Intellectual Property

Dimagi retains all rights, title, and interest in and to the Services and related technology. The Customer retains all rights to Customer Data submitted to the Services.

Indemnification, Disclaimers, and Limitation of Liability

Standard enterprise SaaS indemnification, disclaimer of warranties, and limitation of liability terms apply. Dimagi's aggregate liability is limited to amounts paid by the Customer in the twelve months preceding the claim.

Disputes

US customers: disputes resolved by AAA arbitration in Massachusetts. International customers: disputes resolved under UNCITRAL rules in New York. Class actions are not permitted under this agreement.

Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.
