Transparency in Coverage

This page provides information about machine-readable files made available in response to the federal Transparency in Coverage Rule. The files are hosted by Dimagi's health plan administrator.

View machine-readable files on Cigna's website

This link leads to the machine-readable files that are made available in response to the federal Transparency in Coverage Rule and includes negotiated service rates and out-of-network allowed amounts between health plans and healthcare providers. The machine-readable files are formatted to allow researchers, regulators, and application developers to more easily access and analyze data.
