Reimagining Tuberculosis Treatment and Care: Putting People at the Center of Tech Innovation

Jackie Huh of the Stop TB Partnership and Dr. Kelly Collins of SureAdhere on Reimagining TB Care, a 360-degree, people-centered approach to one of the world's most fatal infectious diseases.

Listen on Spotify Apple Podcasts YouTube Amazon Music

About this episode

Tuberculosis ranks as the second most fatal infectious disease after COVID-19. It is a widespread contagion and a pervasive public health emergency that requires patients to undergo intensive treatment regimes. The Stop TB Partnership, a hosted entity of the United Nations Office for Project Services (UNOPS), is a collective force working to transform the fight against TB globally.

In this episode, Jackie Huh, one of the initiators behind Stop TB's Reimagining TB Care initiative, explains how it addresses the intricate challenges of combating tuberculosis through a comprehensive 360-degree, people-centered approach. She is joined by Dr. Kelly Collins, co-creator of SureAdhere's VDOT, to discuss what it takes to design TB treatment and care around the people living it.

In this episode

  • Reimagining TB Care: how Stop TB's initiative rethinks treatment around the people living through it
  • A 360-degree, people-centered approach to one of the world's most fatal infectious diseases
  • Inside the Stop TB Partnership, a UNOPS-hosted collective force transforming the global fight against TB
  • SureAdhere's VDOT: video-supported treatment adherence, with co-creator Dr. Kelly Collins
  • The weight of intensive treatment regimes and why patient experience has to come first
  • Putting people at the center: bringing technology and care together for TB patients
