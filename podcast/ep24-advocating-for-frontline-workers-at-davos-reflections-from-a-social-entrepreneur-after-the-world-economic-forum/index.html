Advocating for Frontline Workers at Davos: Reflections from a Social Entrepreneur after the World Economic Forum

Fresh from the World Economic Forum Annual Meeting in Davos, Dimagi co-founder Jonathan Jackson shares candid reflections on the biggest problems we face and the biggest bets we need to make to create a better world.

About this episode

Returning from the World Economic Forum Annual Meeting in Davos, Dimagi co-founder Jonathan Jackson sits down to share his candid reflections and takeaways from the gathering.

Through the lens of a social entrepreneur, Jonathan offers his stance on some of the biggest problems we are facing today, and on the biggest bets he believes we need to make to create a better world.

In this episode

  • Reflections from Davos: candid takeaways from the World Economic Forum Annual Meeting
  • A social entrepreneur's lens on the conversations and priorities shaping the global agenda
  • The biggest problems we are facing today, as Jonathan sees them
  • The biggest bets we need to make to create a better world
  • Advocating for frontline workers on a global stage
