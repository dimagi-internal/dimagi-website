The Somatic Edge: Leading with Capacity in a Stress-Filled World

Somatic coach Franzi Gonder on how understanding the body builds the resilience and presence that leadership demands.

  • Leadership
Listen on Spotify Apple Podcasts Amazon Music

About this episode

High performers are taught to push through stress, but Franzi Gonder argues that the body keeps the score, and that learning to read it is a leadership skill in its own right. The renowned somatic leadership coach and entrepreneur joins hosts Amie Vaccaro and Jonathan Jackson to explore how presence and capacity are actually built.

Franzi shares her personal journey into somatic work and unpacks the pervasive beginner's mindset in high-performance environments and its impact on growth. She introduces the Still Point exercise as a practical tool for grounding during challenging times, and makes the case for co-regulation as a core part of leading teams.

It is a grounded, useful conversation for leaders, high-achievers, and anyone seeking greater presence and sustainable performance.

In this episode

  • What somatic awareness is, and why it matters for leadership
  • The ‘beginner's mindset’ trap in high-performance cultures
  • The ‘Still Point’ exercise for grounding under stress
  • Co-regulation: how leaders shape the nervous systems of their teams
  • Building resilience and capacity instead of pushing to burnout
