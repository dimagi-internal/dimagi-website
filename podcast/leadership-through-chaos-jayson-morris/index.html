Leading Through Chaos: Grief, Healing, and Emergent Leadership

Leadership coach Jayson Morris on a new playbook for uncertain times: navigating grief and fear while leading with authenticity and resilience.

About this episode

What happens when the leadership skills that once fueled success no longer serve in today's climate of uncertainty? Leadership coach Jayson Morris returns for a candid conversation with Dimagi CEO Jonathan Jackson and host Amie Vaccaro about leading through genuine turbulence.

Jayson unpacks the idea of the leader as healer, the power of embracing emergence over rigid strategic plans, and why slowing down can actually help teams move faster. You will also hear honest reflections on burnout, on balancing boldness with humility, and on the tension between going down swinging and pivoting to new opportunities.

If you are leading a team through turbulent times, this conversation offers practical wisdom and a much-needed reminder that you are not alone.

  • Embracing emergence over rigid strategic plans
  • Why slowing down can help teams move faster
  • Honest talk on burnout, boldness, and humility
