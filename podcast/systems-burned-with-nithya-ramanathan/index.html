About this episode

The old paradigms of global health are shifting fast, and Nithya Ramanathan, Co-founder and CEO of Nexleaf Analytics, sees the moment clearly. As she puts it, the system has been burned down, and this is our moment, a rare chance to redefine how we create impact rather than a crisis to simply weather.

Nithya joins hosts Jonathan Jackson and Amie Vaccaro to talk through Nexleaf's own strategic pivots, including a shift in business model, and what it takes to own outcomes rather than activities. The conversation is candid about the hard choices organizations face when the ground moves beneath them.

It is a clear-eyed, energizing case for treating disruption as an invitation to build something better, with the discipline to measure what actually changes for people.
