Funders: The Power to Change the System

Mulago Foundation's Dr. Kevin Starr on why broken funding models are a ‘much needed reality bath,’ and how funders can back what actually scales.

  • Global Development
Listen on Spotify Apple Podcasts Amazon Music

About this episode

What comes next in a world where old funding models are breaking down? Our guest, Dr. Kevin Starr, CEO of the Mulago Foundation, argues this shift is not a crisis so much as a much needed reality bath. After a personal tragedy moved him from medicine into philanthropy, Kevin built one of the most respected impact-focused foundations in the field.

In this conversation we explore Mulago's distinctive model of backing scalable solutions and the leaders behind them, including the Rainer Arnhold Fellows program, and examples like Dr. Nick Laing's OneDay Health, a nurse-in-a-room primary care model in Uganda.

It is a sharp, opinionated take on what funders should actually reward, and on the power they hold to change the system.

Dr. Kevin Starr, Mulago Foundation
