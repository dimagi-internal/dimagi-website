Benefits

We care about our team's happiness

We offer competitive, globally-minded benefits designed for a distributed team that makes a real difference in the world.

Excellent Health Insurance

Outstanding medical plan, fully covered for you and your family.

Flexible Work Policy

Dimagi offers employees flexible work hours and locations.

Generous Time Off

Recharge with 30 PTO days and unlimited sick days.

Home Office Benefit

A generous stipend to set up your home office that resets every three years.

Great Parental Leave Policy

12 weeks of leave for primary caregivers, six weeks for non-primary caregivers.

Prepare for Retirement

A 401k in the US, EPF in India, and Retirement Fund in South Africa, all with company matching.

Professional Development

Access to a professional development benefit to advance your career at Dimagi.

Transparent Salary Structure

A standard, open, global pay-scale that is non-negotiable.

Employee Stock Options

You will have a stake in the future success of Dimagi.

Equal Opportunity

Dimagi is an Equal Opportunity Employer. All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, pregnancy, genetic information, disability, status as a protected veteran, or any other protected category under applicable federal, state, and local laws.

If you are interested in applying for employment and need special assistance or reasonable accommodation during the application or interview process for a posted position, please contact us at 617-604-2123 or send an email to accommodations@dimagi.com detailing your request.

For US Applicants

Dimagi maintains an Affirmative Action Plan for the purpose of proactively seeking employment of qualified protected veterans and individuals with disabilities. Upon request, Dimagi will make accessible to you its Affirmative Action Plan for protected veterans and individuals with a disability. If you are interested, please contact us at 617-649-2214 or send an email to aaprequests@dimagi.com. We will then schedule a time for you to review the Affirmative Action Plan during normal business hours, which are Monday through Friday 9:00 am to 3:00 pm Eastern time zone. Dimagi's corporate office is located at 585 Massachusetts Ave, Cambridge, MA 02139.
