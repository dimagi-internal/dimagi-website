Mission & Vision

Driving lasting change through technology

Everything we build, every partnership we form, and every program we support is guided by a clear sense of purpose.

Our Vision
A world where all people have access to the services they need to thrive.
Our Mission
To build and scale sustainable, high-impact digital solutions that amplify frontline work.

Dimagi is a B Corp and a certified Benefit Corporation. At least 50% of people lack access to essential health services. We help bridge this gap by empowering Frontline Workers to deliver critical services to communities in need.

What We Stand For

Our four pillars

We hold ourselves to a high standard. Every product and program we work on must meet these four criteria.

01
Improves Wellbeing

We seek ideas that have the potential to ambitiously and provably improve wellbeing.

02
Inclusive

We design to reach the most underserved populations, especially in technically challenging contexts.

03
Robust

We focus on building robust technology that meets the highest standards with a long-lasting foundation.

04
Market Driven

We balance merit and market. We ensure that all work has a path to scale via the market.

Our Story

Two decades of building what works

Dimagi's story on becoming a leading social impact tech company is one of resilience and well-timed pivots, combined with an unshakable commitment to positive impact on our end users and their communities. Hear our story on our podcast.

2002
Dimagi founded based on desire to use technology for underserved populations.
2008
CommCare flagship software platform created.
2010
Professional services team created for digital health applications.
2013
CommCare offered as Software-as-a-Service, making the platform accessible to organizations of every size around the world.
2022
CDC publishes non-inferiority eDOT study in NYC; SureAdhere acquired.
