Our Why

At least 50% of people lack access to essential health services. Everything we build, every partnership we form, and every program we support is guided by a clear sense of purpose.

Our Vision
A world where all people have access to the services they need to thrive.
Our Mission
To build and scale sustainable, high-impact digital solutions that amplify frontline work.
BCORP

Dimagi is a Certified B Corporation and a Benefit Corporation. We balance purpose and profit, and hold ourselves accountable to the communities we serve. Read our Annual Benefit Report.

What We Stand For

Our four pillars

To create and scale the most social impact through technology, we evaluate every product and program we work on against four pillars.

Improves Wellbeing

We seek ideas with the potential to ambitiously and provably improve wellbeing.

Inclusive

We design to reach the most underserved populations, even in challenging contexts.

Robust

We build robust technology that meets the highest standards on a lasting foundation.

Market Driven

We balance merit and market, ensuring all work has a path to scale via the market.

Our Story

Two decades of building what works

Dimagi's story on becoming a leading social impact tech company is one of resilience and well-timed pivots, combined with an unshakable commitment to positive impact on our end users and their communities. Hear our story on our podcast.

2002
Dimagi founded based on a desire to use technology to serve underserved populations.
2008
CommCare flagship software platform created.
2010
Professional services team created for digital health applications.
2013
CommCare offered as Software-as-a-Service, making the platform accessible to organizations of every size around the world.
2022
CDC publishes non-inferiority eDOT study in NYC; SureAdhere acquired.
