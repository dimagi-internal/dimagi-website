Our Values

The values that guide us

At Dimagi, we care deeply not only about what we do and the impact we have, but also about how we do it. These five values are our operating manual, and they shape every decision we make.

See open roles About Dimagi

Our values serve as our operating manual, defining how we get great work done together.

They are not posters on a wall. We hire for them, promote for them, and hold ourselves and each other accountable to them every day, across every office and time zone.

Hear them in our own words. In a series on our High-Impact Growth podcast, Dimagi CEO Jonathan Jackson is joined by team members to unpack each value and what it means to us in practice.
Value 01

Impact, team, profit.
In that order.

This guides everything we do as a company. Impact, team, and profit create a virtuous flywheel, and keeping them in that order is how we make decisions, hire people, and hold ourselves accountable.

Value 02

Be bold but humble

We take ambitious swings at hard problems while staying grounded, open to feedback, and willing to be wrong. Confidence and humility are not opposites; we need both to do our best work.

Listen to the podcast
Value 03

Lead with empathy and respect

We center the people we serve and treat every colleague and partner with care. This principle shapes how we work with partners and how we collaborate internally, and it is how we keep a supportive, respectful environment for everyone.

Listen to the podcast
Value 04

Evolve by openly learning and sharing

We learn in the open, share what works and what does not, and keep improving together. Transparency is how a global, distributed team gets smarter faster.

Listen to the podcast
Value 05

Embrace complexity for impact

We lean into hard, messy problems, because that is where lasting impact lives. Real-world frontline work is rarely simple, and we are at our best when we meet that complexity head on.

Listen to the podcast
