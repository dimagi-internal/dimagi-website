Africa Community Health

Ethiopia eCHIS

The Health Ministry of Ethiopia selected CommCare as the national electronic Community Health Information System platform, now supporting community health workers across the country in one of the most ambitious national digital health deployments in Africa.

CommCare underpins the Health Extension Worker program, enabling supervisors to monitor case loads, track household registrations, and improve quality of care at the last mile.

United States Infectious Disease

New York City TB Program

SureAdhere's video-observed therapy platform transformed TB treatment delivery in New York City, replacing burdensome daily in-person clinic visits with asynchronous video submission. The landmark study with NYC Health + Hospitals set a new evidence benchmark for TB care.

Results were published in MMWR and directly influenced updated CDC guidance on video DOT as a preferred delivery model for TB treatment.

96%
TB treatment completion rate in the landmark NYC study
United States Behavioral Health

Colorado Behavioral Health

Dimagi partnered with the Colorado Behavioral Health Administration to modernize digital case management for behavioral health programs statewide. The project replaced fragmented paper-based workflows with a unified CommCare system used by case managers across county programs.

The new system reduced documentation time per client, improved supervisor visibility, and enabled the agency to track program outcomes in real time.

State-wide
Digital case management modernization across Colorado

Sierra Leone COVID-19 Response

CommCare supported Sierra Leone's national COVID-19 response, enabling rapid data collection across frontline health workers during a critical public health emergency. The system was deployed within weeks and allowed supervisors to monitor case investigation status and resource needs in real time.

The offline-capable mobile application was particularly critical in areas with unreliable internet, ensuring no data gaps in the national surveillance system.

Rapid
Deployment within weeks of pandemic declaration
United States Community Health

Martha's Vineyard

Dimagi empowers community health workers to provide top-quality care to the most vulnerable residents of Martha's Vineyard, an island community with limited healthcare access. CommCare enables care coordinators to manage client records, track referrals, and ensure follow-through on care plans.

The program demonstrates that the same platform powering national eCHIS deployments in Africa can be right-sized for community health programs in the United States.

CHW
Community health worker program serving vulnerable island residents
India Maternal Health

India RMNCH+A Program

CommCare supports maternal and child health programs across India through partnerships with government health ministries and implementing partners working under the National Health Mission. The RMNCH+A program covers antenatal care, immunization, and postnatal follow-up across multiple states.

ASHA workers use CommCare offline-capable mobile apps to register pregnancies, track immunization schedules, and document household visits, with data syncing to state HMIS systems.

Multi-state
Maternal and child health program deployment across India
