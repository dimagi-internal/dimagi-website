WHAT WE DELIVER

Full-lifecycle services for frontline programs

From first design to long-term sustainability, our specialists cover every capability a program needs, so you can launch quickly, grow with confidence, and ultimately run the system on your own.

Program & solution design

We map real frontline workflows and shape them into a solution your teams will actually use, grounded in two decades of what works in the field.

App & data architecture

Secure, customizable applications and a data architecture built for scale, interoperability, and the realities of low-connectivity environments.

Implementation & rollout

From pilot to nationwide deployment, we handle rollout planning, supervision tools, and the logistics of getting technology into thousands of hands.

Training & capacity building

We transfer skills, not dependency, equipping your staff to manage, adapt, and own the system so it keeps delivering without us.

Monitoring, evaluation & research

Dashboards, data quality systems, and rigorous evaluation, often alongside our Research & Data team, so you can prove impact and act on evidence.

Long-term support & sustainability

Maintainable systems and ongoing support designed for handover, keeping programs resilient and running long after our engagement ends.

HOW WE WORK

A proven path from pilot to national scale

Every engagement follows the same arc, tuned to where your program is today. We meet you at any stage and take you to the next one.

Phase 01

Launch

We design and configure your solution around real frontline workflows, then get it into the hands of workers quickly. App design, data architecture, and rollout planning are handled by specialists who have done it hundreds of times before.

Phase 02

Grow

We help you turn a working pilot into a maturing program, iterating on the application, strengthening data quality, and training your teams so adoption deepens and your evidence base grows.

Phase 03

Scale

We build the internal capacity and maintainable systems your teams need to own, run, and sustain the program, so impact continues long after our partnership ends.
