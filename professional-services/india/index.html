HOW WE WORK

Built for India's frontline reality

Frontline health workers in India often operate in low-bandwidth environments, across many languages, and with constrained device budgets. CommCare is designed for exactly these conditions.

  • ✓Full offline functionality. Apps work without internet and sync when connectivity is restored, critical for rural frontline workers.
  • ✓Multi-language support. Apps can be configured in Hindi, Bengali, Telugu, Tamil, and other regional languages to reach workers in their own language.
  • ✓System integration. CommCare can connect to national and state health management information systems, reducing duplicate data entry.
  • ✓No-code configuration. Program officers can adapt workflows without requiring software developers.
