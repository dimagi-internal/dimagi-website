The Research & Data Team

The Research and Data (RAD) team conducts research on digital solutions intended to improve the health and quality of life of individuals living in low-resource, underserved communities around the world. Utilizing a mixed-methods approach, we build and evaluate digital tools and data analytics to inform our understanding of how digital tools are used and/or generate useful insights for improving healthcare programs.

Our research aims to advance the field of digital development through rigorous investigation of how current and emerging technologies, including AI and Large Language Models, are used and how they can be improved. Progress in digital development depends not only on validating digital technologies but also the generation of programmatic insights that can go towards the improvement of digital solutions.

Selected Publications

RAD collaborates closely with subject matter experts across a variety of public health domains, from more than a dozen universities and academic medical centers. Together we study the use and impact of digital technology on health and related socio-behavioral outcomes. Through these collaborations, RAD seeks to share learnings and research studies with the broader health, ICT4D, and informatics communities.
