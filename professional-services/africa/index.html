What we do across Africa

Far more than a single program

Highlight 01  ·  Community health at national scale

Government-owned eCHIS, built on CommCare

Across Africa, governments are digitizing community health at the national level. Dimagi offers a new, affordable, and better model for the electronic Community Health Information System (eCHIS), built on CommCare, the platform already trusted to run community health programs reaching millions.

A lower total cost of ownership than custom-built systems frees ministry budget for the frontline, not the software, while keeping the program in government hands.
