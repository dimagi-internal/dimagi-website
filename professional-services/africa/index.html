A SUSTAINABLE MODEL

Built for governments to own and run

Our focus is on sustainable program impact. We help ministries build the internal capacity and maintainable systems they need so the program continues long after our engagement ends.

  • ✓Cloud-based SaaS. Deployed as a managed service, with no heavy professional-services contract required to get started.
  • ✓No-code configuration. Government teams build and adapt their own workflows without writing software.
  • ✓Data ownership. Ministries own their data, with local export and storage options.
  • ✓Scalable infrastructure. Built on a proven, open-source platform that already scales to national programs.
