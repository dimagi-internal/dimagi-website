Press & Media

Dimagi is a global social enterprise founded in 2002 by Jonathan Jackson and Dr. Vikram Sheel Kumar. The company powers impactful frontline work through scalable digital solutions and services with teams across the United States, India, South Africa, Senegal, and globally.

About Dimagi

Company Overview

Vision

A world where all people have access to the services they need to thrive.

Products

CommCare

Digital platform for frontline workers deployed in 130+ countries, used by over one million workers for client tracking, service delivery, and data collection.

SureAdhere

Patient support platform using Video Directly Observed Therapy (VDOT) for medication adherence monitoring and remote care.

Connect

Links funding to verified service delivery at the frontline through a network of locally-led organizations.

Open Chat Studio

Open-source platform for building and deploying LLM-powered chatbots across web and messaging platforms.
