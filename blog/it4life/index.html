IT4LIFE

A digital tools partner using human-centered design to improve the wellbeing of humans, animals and ecosystems.

IT4LIFE partners with organizations to build data collection and service delivery mobile applications.

IT4LIFE develops and integrates digital tools to improve the impact of humanitarian and development programs. Founded in 2017, IT4LIFE has delivered over 100 projects in 20 countries.

By using human-centered design, IT4LIFE works hand-in-hand with partners to develop integrated data collection and service delivery mobile applications to improve the wellbeing of humans, animals and ecosystems.
