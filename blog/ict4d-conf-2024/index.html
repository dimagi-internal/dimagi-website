Dimagi to Exhibit and Host Advanced CommCare Training at ICT4D Conference 2024

Join Dimagi at the ICT4D Conference 2024 in Accra, Ghana, for our booth and an Advanced CommCare Training session.

D
Dimagi Feb 2024 2 min read
Share LinkedIn X

Dimagi will exhibit and host Advanced CommCare Training at the ICT4D Conference 2024 in Accra, Ghana.

We’re thrilled to announce our participation at this year’s ICT4D Conference in Accra, Ghana, taking place on March 18th and 19th, 2024. Dimagi will be out in force with a team of eight, showcasing our presence through a booth and offering an Advanced CommCare Training session.

About the ICT4D Conference

Established by Catholic Relief Services, ICT4D stands as the premier cross-sector forum on digital development, dedicated to promoting the impactful use of technology for global betterment by advancing knowledge and capacity in ICT4D. This year’s agenda promises to be as inspiring as ever, spanning two full days of engaging discussions centered around three key themes that resonate deeply with Dimagi’s ethos, and drive much of our work:

  • Digital Approaches to Wicked Problems
  • Data Innovations for Impact
  • Local Leadership for a Digital Future

Advanced CommCare Training

In the spirit of capacity building, the conference will culminate with a dedicated “Training Day & Executive Session” on March 21st. Among the offerings will be the Advanced CommCare Training, led by Dimagi’s Erin Quinn, Senior Director of Customer Success, and Rishabh Rath, Director of Partnerships. Erin and Rishabh have meticulously crafted a hands-on, in-depth workshop focusing on honing data optimization skills. This training presents an excellent opportunity to learn from Dimagi’s seasoned CommCare experts. Attendees will witness a Cash and Voucher Assistance (CVA) demo-app in action, alongside engaging in hands-on sessions and live demonstrations to grasp crucial features.

Connect with us in Accra

Are you or your colleagues attending this year? If so, we’d love to connect! Swing by our booth to meet some of our experienced team members, each a valuable resource on frontline data collection, global health service delivery, and a myriad of other ICT4D topics.

Alternatively, sign up for the Advanced CommCare Training session. Feel free to reach out to us in advance at info@dimagi.com to ensure we have the chance to meet during the conference.
