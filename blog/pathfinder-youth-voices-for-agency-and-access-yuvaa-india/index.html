Pathfinder: Youth Voices for Agency and Access (YUVAA), India

Dimagi is partnering with Pathfinder on the YUVAA program in India to support young couples in making informed family planning decisions.

D
Dimagi Jan 2022 1 min read
Share LinkedIn X

Pathfinder’s YUVAA program in India.

Dimagi is partnering with Pathfinder for the Youth Voices for Agency and Access (YUVAA) program in India to build a digital agent that will engage newly married couples and first-time parents and support them in their decision-making process to adopt family planning as a lifestyle choice.

Pathfinder’s YUVAA program is intended to reach 600,000 young married couples in ten districts across the Bihar and Maharashtra states. The project’s goal is to encourage healthy contraceptive behaviors, improved choice, and informed decision-making on family planning and healthy timing and spacing of pregnancy.
