Dimagi to host its inaugural CommCare Enterprise Summit to drive Impact Delivery

Dimagi’s first-ever CommCare Enterprise Summit brings 16 SaaS Enterprise partners to Washington DC to learn, share, and co-create.

EQ
Erin Quinn Aug 2023 3 min read
Share LinkedIn X

Dimagi’s inaugural CommCare Enterprise Summit takes place September 19 to 21 in Washington DC.

Dimagi, a leading organization dedicated to providing access to essential services for all, is proud to announce its first-ever CommCare Enterprise Summit.

Taking place from September 19 to 21 in Washington DC, the CommCare Enterprise Summit is a remarkable opportunity to bring together 16 of our software as a service (SaaS) CommCare Enterprise partners. These internationally acclaimed organizations are independently building and rolling out digital solutions across the globe, reaching their collective 1.2 billion beneficiaries in the past year.

These Enterprise Partners include Catholic Relief Services (CRS), Childfund, GOAL, Global Communities, International Rescue Committee (IRC), Mercy Corps (MC), MSI Reproductive Choices (MSI), One Acre Fund (OAF), Partners in Health (PIH), Save the Children International (SCI), Sightsavers, Technoserve (TNS), Trocaire, The Tula Foundation, Welthungerhilfe (WHH), and World Vision International (WVI).

Overview of the CommCare Enterprise Summit

With more than half of the world’s population lacking access to essential services, Dimagi aims to bridge this gap and do so sustainably. To explore these challenges and celebrate the success of its partners, Dimagi has organized the first CommCare Enterprise Summit.

What attendees can expect from the summit

Over the course of three days, representatives from invited organizations will engage in meaningful learning, sharing, and collaboration among Dimagi team members and peers. We’ll hear how these organizations are designing their digital strategies, and how we can collectively build CommCare to advance their impact. We’ll work to ensure that all attendees leave the Summit armed with new knowledge, skills, and expertise to amplify their digital programs.

Learn

In our inaugural Summit, we will dive into an agenda rich in topics that the attendees will collaboratively set based on their expressed areas of interest, from best practices related to training and roll out, to setting up their own tiered support structure, and diving into CommCare’s roadmap and new Enterprise features. All attendees leave the Summit armed with new knowledge, skills, and expertise to run better digital programs.

Share

In addition to learning new ways to improve their CommCare programs, a key focus of the Enterprise Summit will be to connect with likeminded Enterprise organizations as well as Dimagi team members. By spending three days together, we hope to foster collaboration, learning, and networking among Summit participants to hear and learn from each other.

Co-Create

The Dimagi team is excited to work with and hear from our Enterprise partners to collectively understand and share how these acclaimed organizations are thinking about their digital strategies. This sense of community will provide attendees with an extended network of colleagues they can rely on for advice and support when making decisions about their own CommCare journey.

Dimagi is thrilled to host this inaugural event and looks forward to welcoming the Enterprise partners in Washington DC. Together, Dimagi and its partners will work towards achieving the vision of a world where all people have access to essential services to thrive.

Sharing rich learnings from the summit

At Dimagi, we believe in the power of knowledge sharing and inclusivity. That’s why we are committed to ensuring that the valuable insights and knowledge gained from the summit are shared with a wider audience.

Over the coming months, we encourage you to follow Dimagi’s LinkedIn channel, monthly newsletter, and our official CommCare SaaS Enterprise Summit page. We will be sharing the insights and content generated from the Enterprise Summit, and hope that you’ll follow along and share their perspective.

Want to learn more about the benefits of being a CommCare Enterprise Partner? Visit our CommCare for Enterprise page.
