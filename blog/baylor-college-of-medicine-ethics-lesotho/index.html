Baylor College of Medicine – ETHICS Lesotho

Expanding a CommCare-based client tracking tool to the final two districts in Lesotho ahead of national deployment.

D
Dimagi Jan 2020 1 min read
Share LinkedIn X

Baylor College of Medicine’s Children’s Foundation Lesotho

The Baylor College of Medicine’s Children’s Foundation Lesotho is working with Dimagi to expand the use of a CommCare-based client tracking tool to two additional districts in the country.

Supporting more than 500 mobile users across these new districts, the application will support MCH, TB, and HIV departments by tracking appointments, sending SMS reminders to patients, transferring patients within and between hospitals, and reporting on a select set of outcomes.

These are the final two districts to receive access to the app before it will have been deployed nationally.
