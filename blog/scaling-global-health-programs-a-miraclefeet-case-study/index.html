Scaling Global Health Programs: A MiracleFeet Case Study

How MiracleFeet scaled clubfoot treatment from 180 to over 500 clinics by automating its CommCare-to-Salesforce data pipeline, giving more than 22,000 staff hours a year back to patient care.

SS
Sarah Strauss Apr 2026 3 min read
Share LinkedIn X

A child receiving clubfoot care through a MiracleFeet-supported clinic.

MiracleFeet partners with local health systems to treat children born with clubfoot, a common and highly treatable birth condition that affects nearly 200,000 children each year. A CommCare partner since 2016, MiracleFeet has grown from 180 clinics to more than 500. With that growth came a defining challenge: turning a rising volume of field data into faster, life-changing care.

From data collection to automated workflows

As MiracleFeet grew from 180 clinics to over 500, the volume of data became a massive opportunity, and a massive challenge. To reach their goal of treating 17,000+ new children annually, they needed to bridge the gap between field-level patient interactions and their central Salesforce monitoring and evaluation (M&E) system.

By partnering with OpenFn to automate their data pipeline, MiracleFeet ensures that field-level data from CommCare reaches Salesforce in real time. This shift removes the friction of manual entry and human error, empowering the MiracleFeet team to monitor patient progress globally and provide life-changing interventions the second a child’s treatment falls behind schedule.

“CommCare has been a foundational partner for us since 2016. The platform’s reliability and powerful offline-first case management are critical.”

Clifford Hakimi, Associate Director of Digital Systems, MiracleFeet

The results: saving time to save lives

The shift to a fully integrated ecosystem has transformed MiracleFeet’s operational efficiency. By leveraging the strengths of both CommCare and OpenFn, the organization has achieved:

  • Real-time visibility into treatment quality across 500+ global clinics.
  • 250,000+ patient visits tracked annually.
  • 22,020 hours of manual staff time saved every year.
Manual staff time saved
22,020 hrs / year

Automating the CommCare-to-Salesforce pipeline gave more than 22,000 hours back to MiracleFeet’s team every year, time now spent advancing patient care instead of re-keying data by hand.

With clean data flowing from the field to headquarters in real time, MiracleFeet can act the moment a child’s treatment falls behind, ensuring no patient is lost to follow-up as the program continues to scale toward its next milestone.

For a deeper look at the architecture and numbers behind the integration, OpenFn published a full technical case study and ROI analysis.
