The new, free Case Management Course on Dimagi Academy.

Imagine you’re a frontline worker preparing for a visit. Before heading out, you open your phone and instantly see notes from your last visit: a sick child who needed follow-up, a farmer whose fertilizer order arrived, a client whose legal referral was just completed. You’re not starting from scratch, you’re picking up exactly where you left off. This is case management in action.

Why case management changes everything

Case management transforms how frontline workers deliver essential services by connecting every interaction into a continuous story. Instead of treating each visit as isolated, CommCare tracks key details across time, building a complete picture of each person you serve.

For frontline workers, it means no more shuffling through papers or relying on memory. Your caseload is organized with visual indicators showing who needs urgent attention, and when you open a form, the client’s history is already there.

For organizations, it means tracking individuals, households, or entities over time with rich data that shows real impact, connecting workflows across programs and reducing duplicate data entry.

For the people you serve, it means more personalized care from workers who arrive prepared, remember their circumstances, and can act faster when needs are urgent.

Introducing Dimagi Academy’s newest course

We’re thrilled to announce the launch of Dimagi Academy’s brand-new Case Management Course, created in response to the growing need for comprehensive, accessible training on this powerful feature. This interactive, self-paced training simplifies even the most complex concepts into short, easy-to-follow lessons with real-world CommCare examples you can apply right away.

By the end of the course, you’ll understand:

  • What case management is and why it matters.
  • How it improves your app’s performance and user experience.
  • The lifecycle of a case, from creation to closure.

Designed for beginners, valuable for everyone
