UNDP – Financial Education Application

Dimagi built a mobile application for frontline workers under the UNDP and Cameroon's PAREC project to deliver financial training and support savings groups.

D
Dimagi Sep 2021 1 min read
Share LinkedIn X

Dimagi partnered with the UNDP and the Cameroonian government on the economic resilience support project (PAREC).

In contribution to the national strategy for inclusive finance, the Cameroonian government and the UNDP launched the economic resilience support project (PAREC) to reduce poverty and inequality at the local level. The project aims to offer socio-economic opportunities to vulnerable populations, including women and young people, in order to strengthen their economic resilience.

Dimagi was selected to develop a mobile application for frontline workers to provide financial training to beneficiaries and to manage saving group activities monitored by microfinance institutions, as well as raise awareness of COVID-19 information.
