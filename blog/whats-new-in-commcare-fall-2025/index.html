What’s New in CommCare: Fall 2025 Product Updates

Highlights from the Fall 2025 webinar: an AI support bot, bulk case editing, smart performance alerts, new cloud regions, and a more affordable Standard plan, built to help leaner teams do more with less.

SS
Sarah Strauss Oct 2025 5 min read
Share LinkedIn X

Product highlights from the Fall 2025 What’s New in CommCare webinar.

This is a challenging time for the global health and development sectors. With leaner teams, tighter budgets, and growing complexity, your work is more critical than ever, and your tools need to be more supportive than ever. Our Fall 2025 CommCare updates were built around exactly that.

Leaner teams, tighter budgets

On October 7th, we gathered with partners from around the world for our “What’s New in CommCare: Fall 2025” webinar. As our Director of Technology and Product, Kai Cowger, put it: CommCare’s power is meaningless if it’s difficult to unlock. That’s why we’ve made it a priority to refine the core platform so any organization, regardless of size or technical capacity, can realize value from day one. If you missed the live session, you can watch the full recording here.

Doing more with leaner teams

As workloads grow and program staff take on increasingly technical roles, these updates make it easier for smaller teams to manage their digital tools with confidence:

  • The CommCare Companion: a brand-new AI-powered support bot that gives instant, step-by-step guidance right inside the app builder, exactly when you’re stuck.
  • Bulk case data editing: clean, update, and manage thousands of cases directly and safely within CommCare, ending the tedious export-and-reimport cycle.
  • A free case management course: a new free case management course on Dimagi Academy, plus a series of enhancements, makes the platform’s core strength easier for everyone to master.
  • Smart Performance Alerts: real-time guidance in the App Builder that warns you before an app slows down, so teams build faster, more reliable apps without being performance experts.

Navigating growing complexity

As digital and regulatory environments grow more complex, we’re lowering technical barriers so your vital work isn’t slowed down:

  • New cloud locations: to meet stricter data residency requirements, CommCare projects can now be hosted in the United States, the EU, or India.
  • AI-based language translations: the entire CommCare HQ interface is now available in 9 key languages, including Arabic, French, Spanish, and Hindi.

Maximizing impact with tighter budgets

With funding cuts across the sector, every tool must demonstrate a clear return on investment:

  • A more accessible Standard plan: the CommCare Standard plan now provides robust data collection, case management, and top-tier security at just $100/month.
  • One platform for every use case: new template apps for Cash & Voucher Assistance and Child Sponsorship show how CommCare brings programs, sectors, and workflows together on a single platform.
