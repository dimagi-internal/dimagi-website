Supporting Our Partners Through Change

As USAID restructuring reshapes global development, here’s how Dimagi is supporting partners through the transition, from pricing to networks to what comes next.

GJ
Gillian Javetski Feb 2025 3 min read Global Health
Share LinkedIn X

Supporting partners as the global development funding landscape shifts.

On January 20th, the United States government announced the start of a USAID restructuring that has since had a significant impact on the global development industry. In the immediate term, our focus is on supporting our partners as they navigate new challenges. Here’s what Dimagi is doing.

Making CommCare more affordable

With funding cuts, organizations face intense budget pressure. To help CommCare users, including locally-led organizations, sustain their vital work, we’re reducing the price of CommCare’s Standard Software Plan from $250 to $100 a month. If you’d like to learn more or sign up, share your information here and we’ll keep you updated as the change takes effect.

Supporting our networks

Many organizations are facing urgent funding gaps. For partners that need to make the case to funders, we’re offering letters of support to ensure continued access to CommCare, which has already helped multiple programs remain operational through stop-work orders. If a letter of support would help your program, email info@dimagi.com.

These changes have also left many professionals navigating an uncertain job market. We’ll continue to post opportunities to the CommCare Job Board, and for those looking to build new skills, Dimagi Academy offers free, self-paced courses to deepen your CommCare expertise.

Final thoughts

We’re a technology company with an unshakable belief that digital solutions can maximize the impact of every dollar invested in global health and development, a commitment that sits at the core of our Impact Delivery approach. The road ahead is uncertain, but we’ll be here to navigate the changes alongside our partners.

Stay tuned as we share how we’re adapting and listening, including a new podcast episode, The Future of Aid: Adapting to a Changing Funding Landscape.

Navigating the funding shift?

Sign up to be notified when we launch the lower-priced Standard Plan, and follow along as we share what we learn.
