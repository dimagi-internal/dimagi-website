GiveWell Awards Dimagi Funding to Enhance Child Health Service Delivery in Nigeria

A GiveWell grant of more than $1 million will scale CommCare Connect to deliver over 300,000 child health visits across underserved regions of northern Nigeria.

D
Dimagi Nov 2024 3 min read
Share LinkedIn X

Image credit: World Poultry Foundation, Zambia, 2024

GiveWell has awarded Dimagi a $1+ million grant to scale service delivery via CommCare Connect and cost-effectively improve essential child health service coverage in Nigeria. CommCare Connect builds on Dimagi’s flagship CommCare platform to enable frontline workers to opt in to additional paid, purposeful work through scalable learn-deliver-verify-pay cycles, strengthening and leveraging existing frontline workforces to improve health outcomes.

This funding will enable Dimagi to engage several locally led organizations to deliver the CommCare Connect-Child Health Campaign in targeted, underserved regions of northern Nigeria, scaling efforts through high fidelity replication (versus centralized expansion). Dimagi and our partners will provide more than 300,000 child health visits over the course of 18 months, providing Vitamin A supplementation, oral rehydration solution and zinc, malnutrition screening and promoting vaccination for children under 5.

Child Health Campaign overview

The Child Health Campaign (CCC-CHC) targets high under-five mortality rates due to preventable diseases. FLWs conduct door-to-door visits to register children under five and deliver a range of health services, including:

  • Vitamin A Supplementation: Essential for reducing child mortality.
  • Vaccine Promotion: Encouraging complete immunization schedules, significantly increasing vaccination rates in marginalized communities.
  • ORS and Zinc Distribution: Providing treatments for diarrhea, a leading cause of child mortality.
  • Malnutrition Screening and Referral: Identifying and addressing malnutrition, a critical health issue in many LMICs.

Dimagi has achieved early success with locally led partner organizations implementing CCC-CHC in five countries, reaching over 100,000 children. Dimagi’s approach leverages the experience and trust of locally-led organizations operating in high-need communities to recruit and train FLWs, obtain government permissions, and coordinate community sensitization and campaign logistics. The use of CCC ensures that high-quality, verified services are delivered efficiently as the program scales up.

About Dimagi

Dimagi is a global social enterprise that powers impactful frontline work through scalable digital solutions and services. Since 2002, Dimagi has been guided by a vision of a world where everyone has access to the services they need to thrive. Dimagi is most well-known as the makers of CommCare, the most widely-deployed digital platform for enabling Frontline Workers. Governments and organizations across all sectors use customized mobile, web and SMS applications built on CommCare to deliver services at the frontline. Dimagi is a certified Benefit Corporation with team members in the United States, India, South Africa, Senegal and around the world.

For more information, visit Dimagi’s website to explore their work and the impact of CommCare Connect.

About GiveWell

GiveWell is a nonprofit organization dedicated to finding and recommending outstanding giving opportunities to help donors make impactful, evidence-based decisions. Established in 2007, GiveWell conducts in-depth research and analysis to identify high-impact charities and programs that effectively address critical global health and poverty issues. Their evaluations focus on cost-effectiveness, transparency, and proven outcomes, ensuring that donations achieve the greatest possible impact. By providing detailed reports and recommendations, GiveWell aims to guide donors toward the most effective ways to improve lives and reduce suffering worldwide. For more information, visit GiveWell.
