Scaling Beyond the Wall: Unlocking New CommCare Use Cases for Operational Efficiency

Beyond monitoring and evaluation: how partners are extending CommCare into asset tracking, fleet management, and internal logistics to do more with leaner budgets.

EQ
Erin Quinn Dec 2025 4 min read CommCare
Share LinkedIn X

Extending CommCare beyond monitoring and evaluation into the operational workflows that keep programs running.

Budgets are tighter and teams are leaner, so every digital investment has to work harder. For many organizations, the platform they already trust for monitoring and evaluation is the most under-used asset they own. The opportunity is not another tool; it is unlocking new use cases for the one already in their hands.

The first leap: beyond M&E, into service delivery

The first step in realizing CommCare’s potential is often an organic expansion: leveraging your existing M&E data collection system to encompass core service delivery applications. If your organization hasn’t made this shift yet, it’s the quickest way to unlock immediate value and demonstrate that CommCare scales with your programmatic needs.

Unlocking the next horizon: operational excellence

Once programming use cases are maximized, the next frontier of expansion lies in areas completely outside of traditional program work. This is how partners demonstrate that they are truly scaling past the limitations of single-purpose tools.

While public case studies often focus on clinical impact, innovators in the sector are quietly breaking new ground behind the scenes. This shift leverages CommCare’s robust offline-first architecture and powerful case management features, tracking an ‘entity’ over time, whether that entity is a patient or a pickup truck, to solve persistent, high-value operational challenges, such as:

  • Asset management: Conducting real-time inventory and tracking the status of high-value organizational assets, like phones, laptops, and other in-country hardware, ensuring accountability and longevity.
  • Fleet management: Tracking vehicle maintenance logs, managing fuel consumption, and scheduling service for in-country vehicles, minimizing downtime and cost without requiring a separate, expensive fleet software license.
  • Internal logistics: Digitizing facility inspection checklists, managing office supply requests, or tracking essential infrastructure maintenance, such as generator service, for a more resilient backbone.

Three questions to guide your expansion

The path to unlocking this hidden potential is about strategic integration rather than simple duplication. Before launching a new operational use case, Dimagi recommends asking three self-reflection questions to make sure you maximize your momentum:

1

Is this an opportunity for integration?

If you have an existing tool (like an ERP, HRIS, or dedicated fleet management system), CommCare’s ultimate value may not be in replacing it. Instead, think of CommCare as the offline data collection front-end that seamlessly integrates with your existing backend system. This approach preserves your current investments while leveraging CommCare’s power where other systems can’t reach.

2

Does this work have a frontline component?

If yes, you’ve found CommCare’s sweet spot. CommCare is an offline-first data system designed to excel in complex, disconnected environments. Any use case that involves a frontline component, a person in the field or in a remote facility physically inspecting, logging, or managing something, is where CommCare shines and adds unique, scalable value.

3

Are the proposed users ready to be empowered by CommCare?

Success hinges on user-centric design. Unlike trained data collectors, operational staff like drivers or facility managers may have different levels of digital comfort. To unlock this potential, the tool cannot just be a requirement; it must be a resource. Ensuring the workflow is simple and the training is targeted will turn these new users into your biggest champions.

Built for endless potential

CommCare is not a tool you will hit a wall with. It is a highly adaptable, trusted software platform built for complexity, disconnection, and continuous expansion. By extending CommCare into internal operations and logistics, our partners are not just saving money, they are building a more resilient, integrated, and efficient digital ecosystem that continuously unlocks new potential.
