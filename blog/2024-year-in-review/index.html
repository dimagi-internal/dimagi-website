2024 Year-in-Review: Scaling Digital Solutions for Global Impact

A look back at 2024: from 173,638 CommCare users and SureAdhere’s 800,000th video to national government partnerships and impact close to home.

AV
Amie Vaccaro Jan 2025 3 min read Global Health
Share LinkedIn X

A year of scaling digital solutions for global impact.

As we step into 2025, global health and development faces an increasingly uncertain landscape. The need for essential services keeps growing while funding is squeezed, creating a clear mandate for technology companies like Dimagi to deliver significantly more value and impact per dollar. Here’s a look at what we and our partners achieved together in 2024.

CommCare at scale

CommCare users in 2024
173,638

From frontline staff to M&E and program leads across sectors and geographies, with 7,338 new project spaces created over the year.

We partnered with nine governments across Africa committed to digitally transforming service delivery, and hosted our inaugural CommCare Government Summit, bringing together 64 leaders from 11 countries.

SureAdhere, Open Chat Studio, and Connect

SureAdhere, our digital adherence and patient support platform, processed its 800,000th video, helping patients on complex treatment regimens regain freedom and control while saving public health resources. Fifty partners joined Open Chat Studio, our open-source, LLM-based platform for advancing AI in global development.

Health services delivered via CommCare Connect
101,734

Connect lets frontline workers opt into additional work and get paid for it, delivering services from Vitamin A and deworming for under-fives to childhood development counselling for caregivers.

From global to local

Closer to home, Dimagi’s work in Colorado reduced patient wait times for critical Medication Assisted Therapy from days to under five minutes, and enabled the admission of more than 17,000 clients over the year.

The challenges ahead require not just innovative digital solutions but strong partnerships and a deep commitment to the people and frontline staff delivering services. Through our Impact Delivery approach, we’re more dedicated than ever to helping you deliver meaningful results today while building a foundation for tomorrow.

Here’s to the year ahead
